## 🔥 Fire NOC Management System

> ⚡ Digitizing government approval workflows with scalable and transparent architecture.

### 📌 Overview

The **Fire NOC Management System** is a government-based web application designed to digitize and streamline the complete process of obtaining Fire No Objection Certificates (NOC) for building projects.

The system replaces manual workflows with an **online approval system**, ensuring transparency, faster processing, and real-time tracking.

---

### 🚀 Key Features

* 📄 Online Application Submission (CC & OC)
* 🏢 Multi-Level Authority Approval Workflow
* 🔍 Real-Time Application Status Tracking
* 💳 Online Payment Integration
* 📊 Dashboard & Analytics (Service-wise Reports)
* 📑 Automated Receipt Generation
* 🔗 API-Based Data Autofetch (BPMS Integration)
* 📥 Final NOC Upload & Download

---

### 🏗️ Modules

#### 🔹 Commencement Certificate (CC)

* Application Submission by Architect
* Site Inspection by Authority
* Demand Generation & Payment
* Final NOC Approval

#### 🔹 Occupancy Certificate (OC)

* Linked with CC Data (Auto-fetch)
* Multi-Level Review Process
* Payment & Final NOC Approval

---

### ⚙️ Workflow

1. Login to Portal
2. Select Project & Service
3. Fill Application Details
4. Submit Application
5. Authority Inspections & Reviews
6. Payment & Receipt Generation
7. Final NOC Approval & Upload
